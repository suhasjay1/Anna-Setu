Ever wonder how much food is wasted?

In India, an estimated 40% of all food produced is wasted—enough to feed millions every single day.

This isn't just about expired products; it's about perfectly good, nutritious meals from restaurants, events, and hotels that end up in landfills simply due to logistical challenges. We built Anna Setu to solve that problem.

Turn Surplus into Sustenance.

We empower restaurants, hotels, and individuals to fight food waste by redirecting surplus meals to communities in need, animal shelters, and composting initiatives.

The Bridge Between Waste and Want

Anna Setu—meaning "Food Bridge"—was created to solve this logistical challenge. We go beyond just feeding people by recognizing that all surplus food has value. Our platform provides a seamless and transparent way to donate food for human consumption, provide feed for animal shelters, or contribute to a greener planet through our composting program. We ensure that good food nourishes people, animals, and the earth—not landfills.

A Simple Path to a Powerful Impact

1. Donors List Food

Using a simple form on their dashboard, a donor provides details about their surplus food—whether it's fresh meals, groceries, or items for compost—and sets a pickup time.

2. We Find the Right Home

Our admin team receives the request and assigns it. Human-grade food goes to NGOs, animal-grade food to shelters, and compostable waste to our green partners.

3. Volunteers Deliver Hope

The volunteer picks up the donation, performs a quick quality check to ensure safety, and delivers it directly to a registered NGO, shelter, or community kitchen in need.
